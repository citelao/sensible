What is Sensible Security?

Sensible Security is a complete guide to cybersecurity for normal people. It focuses on the real, enduring concepts used by security professionals to equip you with the tools you need to keep you and your family safe.

The Internet is dangerous. It's right to be afraid of it sometimes.

But it's wrong to be afraid of it without understanding it. Sensible Security helps you understand it.
